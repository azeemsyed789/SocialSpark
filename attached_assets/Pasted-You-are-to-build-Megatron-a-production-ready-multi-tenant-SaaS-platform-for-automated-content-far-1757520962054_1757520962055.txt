You are to build Megatron — a production-ready, multi-tenant SaaS platform for automated content farming and performance testing. Megatron must include every feature described below and be delivered as a working MVP: all modules must be functional, no placeholder components.

High-level summary:
Megatron automates multi-platform publishing (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Reddit, Discord), supports unlimited accounts per platform, provides a central content calendar, auto-generates and posts content (text, images, memes, short-form videos), runs controlled A/B tests, scores market-fit, rotates posting patterns to minimize shadow bans, supports proxy posting at scale, provides deep analytics and AI weekly summaries, enables collaboration and approvals, and includes templates & pricing tiers.

1 — Core Requirements (must be fully functional)
Platforms & Posting

Post to TikTok, Instagram (via Buffer webhook/Make.com), YouTube Shorts, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Reddit, Discord.

Connect multiple accounts per platform (unlimited). Account manager UI with status and health indicators.

Publishing quotas configurable per account and per org (defaults below).

Bulk upload and drag/drop assets.

Quotas (default, configurable)

TikTok: 3 videos / day / account

Instagram: 3 posts/reels / day / account

Communities (Reddit/Discord): 2 posts / day / account

LinkedIn: 1–2 posts / day / account

YouTube Shorts/Twitter as configured

Scheduling & Calendar

Central content calendar (month/week/day views), drag & drop reschedule.

Slot metadata: datetime, timezone, platform, account, content type, tagline, campaign, A/B flags.

Bulk CSV / Google Sheets import/export.

AI Content Generation

Gemini (text + multimodal) for:

Long/short captions, FOMO/left-out hooks, hashtag suggestions, and image generation (multimodal).

HeyGen for avatar video generation (scripts from Gemini).

Image generation fallback: use Gemini vision or Stable Diffusion API if preferred.

All generated content must include “left-out / FOMO” undertone when requested.

Auto Rotation & Account Health

Rotation scheduler that varies posting times / sequencing across accounts to reduce shadow ban risk.

Content Variation Tracking to ensure not posting identical content across accounts.

Account Health Monitor (proxy detection, login success rate, posting error rate).

Residential proxy integration + IP rotation + account isolation.

Proxy & Security

Proxy connector layer (support residential & datacenter proxies).

Encrypt credentials/tokens at rest.

Per-account request routing through proxies.

A/B Testing Suite

Create split tests for caption, visual, CTA, or posting time.

Randomization engine assigns controlled variants.

Statistical significance calculator; auto picks winner after threshold.

Visual side-by-side variant comparison with charts.

Market Fit Scoring

Proprietary Market Fit Score combining:

Engagement metrics (views, likes, watch time)

Sentiment analysis (comments)

Growth / velocity (e.g., follower growth for the campaign)

Display single numeric Market Fit Score + breakdown and suggestions.

Analytics & Insights

Unified dashboard with cross-platform metrics: views, likes, comments, watch time, CTR, conversions.

Real-time updates where APIs allow.

Sentiment analysis: emotion detection, topic clustering, trending sentiments.

AI Weekly Summary (auto-generated): highlights, weak points, suggested next experiments.

Performance comparison: heatmaps, trend lines, conversion tracking.

Collaboration & Approval

Workspaces per org/client. Team roles (Owner, Admin, Publisher, Approver, Viewer).

Multi-stage approval flows (draft → review → approved → scheduled).

Comment threads and version history for content edits.

White-label reports and scheduled delivery to clients (PDF/HTML).

Automation & Webhooks

Smart Scheduling: AI suggests optimal times and automates placements.

Content Recycling: reshare top performing posts with automatic variation.

Webhook integrations: when performance thresholds met → trigger actions (e.g., notify, re-promote, post follow-ups).

Zapier + Make.com connectors.

Templates & Use Cases

Ship templates (SaaS Product Launch Hook, B2B Pain Point Reel, UGC Product Review, etc.) with copy, hashtags, and suggested schedule.

Allow custom templates and store in Template Library.

Pricing & Plans (implement gating)

Free: $0 forever — 10 posts/day, 1 workspace, basic analytics, community support (no Market Fit Score, no AI captions)

Pro: $29/month — Unlimited accounts, full analytics, Market Fit Score, AI captions/hashtags, A/B testing

Agency: $149/month — Multi-client management, white-label reports, role permissions, dedicated support
